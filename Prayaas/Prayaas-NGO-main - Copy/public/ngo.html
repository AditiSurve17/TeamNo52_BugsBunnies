OUR MISSION

At UMEED, we are committed to delivering surplus donations to those in need. We believe everyone deserves the chance to lead a healthy and fulfilling life. Our mission is to improve lives by providing access to essential necessities such as food, shelter, education, and healthcare.


We tirelessly identify the pressing needs in our community and develop programs to address them. With the generous support of our donors, we positively impact thousands of lives each year—whether by providing a warm meal or helping a child access education. We are dedicated to making a difference in our community and beyond.
